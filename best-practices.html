Installation Guide & Best Practices

Proper installation is essential for collecting reliable and interpretable thermal data.

Recognizing that financial, logistical, and equipment constraints may limit the adoption of ideal protocols, we offer a tiered framework for addressing and communicating uncertainty.

Tier 1 reflects best-practice recommendations developed by the community, while subsequent tiers represent feasible alternatives when ideal approaches are not possible. To support consistency, comparability, and transparency across studies, we pair technical guidance with clear reporting standards so researchers can effectively communicate potential sources of uncertainty.

Reporting tier designation is required for inclusion in the TIR-ific Network. This shared framework not only ensures transparency and contextual understanding of data quality but also strengthens the credibility of near-surface TIR observations, supports integration across networks, and unlocks broader applications.
