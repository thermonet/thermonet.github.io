Tier Tables

Our tiered framework provides standardized guidance for thermal sensor deployment and processing, recognizing varying constraints.

Read the full framework in the Best Practices paper

Pre-deployment Testing

Tier Description
Tier 1 Blackbody testing at 3 different room temperatures.
Tier 2 Blackbody testing at 1 room temperature.
Tier 3 Waterbath testing at 3 different room temperatures.
Tier 4 Waterbath testing at 1 room temperature.
Tier 5 No prior testing done.

Table 1. Tiers for testing performed prior to deployment. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.

Correction Measurements

Tier Description
Tier 1 Correction measurements co-located (within 1 km) and concurrent (within 10 minutes) and current distance measurement.
Tier 2 Correction measurements mismatched in either space (>1 km) or time (>10 minutes), current or outdated distance.
Tier 3 Correction measurements mismatched in both space (>1 km) and time (>10 minutes), current or outdated distance.
Tier 4 Correction measurements from remote sensing or reanalysis, current or outdated distance.
Tier 5 No correction measurements obtained.

Table 2. Tiers for correction measurements. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.

Contact Vegetation Measurements

Tier Description
Tier 1 Contact vegetation measurements placed on all ROIs and concurrent (within 10 minutes).
Tier 2 Contact vegetation measurements mismatched in either space (not all ROIs) or time (>10 minutes).
Tier 3 Contact vegetation measurements mismatched in both space (not all ROIs) and time (>10 minutes).
Tier 4 Contact vegetation measurements outside camera's FOV, concurrent time (>10 minutes) or not.
Tier 5 No contact vegetation measurements made.

Table 3. Tiers for contact vegetation measurements. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.

Reference Panel

Tier Description
Tier 1 Reference panel(s) (1 or more) placed near ROI(s) with embedded concurrent (within 10 minutes) thermistor measurements and proper mounting.
Tier 2 Reference panel(s) (1 or more) placed near ROI(s) mismatched in either time (>10 minutes) or proper mounting, embedded or not.
Tier 3 Reference panel (1) placed near TIR camera (i.e. boom arm off of the tower) with concurrent (within 10 minutes) thermistor measurements with proper mounting, embedded or not.
Tier 4 Reference panel (1) placed near TIR camera (i.e. boom arm off of the tower) mismatched in either time (>10 minutes) or proper mounting, embedded or not.
Tier 5 No reference panel used.

Table 4. Tiers for reference panel deployment. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.

Non-Contact Measurement

Tier Description
Tier 1 Non-contact measurement co-located (within 1 m) and concurrent (within 10 minutes).
Tier 2 Non-contact measurement mismatched in either space (>1 m) or time (>10 minutes)
Tier 3 Non-contact measurement mismatched in both space (>1 m) and time (>10 minutes).
Tier 4 No non-contact measurements used.

Table 5. Tiers for non-contact measurement of reference panel. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.

Interpretation (RGB Imaging)

Tier Description
Tier 1 RGB camera co-located (within 1 m) and concurrent (within 10 minutes).
Tier 2 RGB camera mismatched in either space (>1 m) or time (>10 minutes)
Tier 3 RGB camera mismatched in both space (>1 m) and time (>10 minutes).
Tier 4 Single RGB image obtained.
Tier 5 No RGB image obtained.

Table 6. Tiers for interpretation measurements. Recommended Best Practice: Tier 1.
